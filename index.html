Abstract

This project presents ELVID, a curated benchmark for infrared‚Äìvisible video fusion under extremely low-light conditions, together with SCFNet, an uncertainty-guided spatio-temporal consistency fusion network. SCFNet integrates spatio-temporal uncertainty analysis, a modality-specific difference-guided mechanism, and a hierarchical expert collaboration strategy to improve temporal stability and preserve fine-grained details. Extensive experiments on ELVID and public datasets demonstrate consistent improvements over competitive baselines.
